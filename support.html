Common questions

Appstinence isn't intercepting my apps. What's wrong?

Appstinence relies on Shortcuts Automations to redirect you when you open a monitored app. If you skipped the Shortcuts setup during onboarding, or didn't set one up for a specific app, the redirect won't fire. Open Appstinence, go to Settings → My Apps → Set up Shortcuts to walk through the setup again.

I subscribed but I'm still seeing the free tier.

Tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings → Purchase. If that doesn't help, force-quit the app and reopen it. If you're still stuck, email us with the Apple Account email you used for the purchase and we'll help.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions. Apple manages all subscriptions. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period; you keep Pro access until then.

Can I get a refund?

Refund requests go through Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request one. Gopher Labs LLC cannot issue refunds directly.

Why does the app need Family Controls / Screen Time access?

Family Controls is the iOS framework that lets Appstinence detect when you open the apps you've chosen to be more mindful about. Without it, the app can't intercept your scrolling. All this data stays on your device. We never see it. See the privacy policy for details.

Does Appstinence collect my data?

No. We don't have a server. Nothing leaves your device. Read more →

The Shortcuts Automation isn't working — the app I open just opens normally.

Three things to check: (1) the Automation is enabled (open the Shortcuts app, find the Automation, make sure it's on), (2) "Run Immediately" is selected (not "Ask Before Running"), (3) the Automation runs Appstinence as the action. The in-app walkthrough covers each step in detail.

Can I use Appstinence on multiple devices?

Yes. Subscriptions tied to your Apple Account work on any iPhone signed into that account, including via Family Sharing if enabled.

Can I export or delete my data?

All Appstinence data lives on your device only. Deleting the app removes all of it. There is nothing to export from a server because we don't have a server.

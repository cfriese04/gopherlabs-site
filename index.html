About

An independent software studio
based in Minnesota.

Gopher Labs is a young company with strong opinions about how software should feel. We build small, focused tools that solve specific problems with care and intention. We don't take venture capital. We don't sell user attention. We don't ship features to chase metrics.

Everything we make is local-first by design. Your data stays on your device. There are no servers reading what you do, no third-party trackers, no advertising. The economics work because the products are good enough to pay for — once.

Approach

One thing at a time.

We work on one product at a time and stay with it until it's right. We don't follow trends. We pick problems that matter to us, then refuse to ship until the result is something we'd hand to someone we respect.

We're not against scale — we're against haste. The right product, well made, finds its audience. We'd rather be slow and good than fast and forgettable.

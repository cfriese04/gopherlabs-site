Privacy Policy

Last updated April 28, 2026

Gopher Labs LLC operates the Appstinence iOS app. This privacy policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: nothing leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Appstinence makes zero network requests. No analytics, no telemetry, no servers, no accounts, no sign-in. We have no way to identify you and no way to contact you unless you reach out to us first.

What stays on your device

  • Screen Time tokens. When you grant Family Controls permission, iOS gives Appstinence opaque tokens that represent the apps you chose to monitor. We cannot read app names from these tokens. They never leave your device.
  • Intervention logs. Counts of "go back" and "open anyway" choices you make at intervention screens. Used locally for stats, streaks, and progress.
  • Settings. Your preferences for intervention types, intensity, daily limits, enabled categories, and other configuration.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active Appstinence Pro subscription, cached locally and verified against Apple's StoreKit.

All of this lives in your device's App Group container, which is sandboxed by iOS and accessible only to Appstinence and its extensions.

Third parties

The only third party involved is Apple, who handles subscription payments via StoreKit and, if you have iCloud Backup enabled, may include Appstinence's local data in your encrypted device backups. Apple's privacy practices apply to those interactions. We never see your payment details and we never see your iCloud data.

Permissions we may request

  • Family Controls. Required for the app to function. Lets Appstinence detect when you open monitored apps.
  • Notifications. Optional. Used only to remind you that you're still scrolling at the time thresholds you've set.

If you deny these permissions, the relevant features simply won't work. We do not pester or punish.

Children

Appstinence is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page and post the new version at gopherlabs.co/privacy.html. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the new policy.
